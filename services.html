Services

What We Do

Nexus delivers signage, environmental graphics, and wayfinding systems designed for complex, active organizations. Our services focus on planning, fabrication, and installation of physical branding elements that require precision, coordination, and dependable execution. We support both one-off needs and large, multi-phase programs, providing consistency and control from concept through installation.

Signage Systems

+

Design, fabrication, and installation of interior and exterior signage systems, including building identification, directional signage, regulatory signage, and custom solutions built to align with brand standards and operational needs.

Environmental & Wall Graphics

+

Large-format wall graphics, murals, vinyl applications, and surface graphics designed to transform interior and exterior spaces through color, imagery, and messaging.

Dimensional & Architectural Displays

+

Custom dimensional lettering, donor and recognition displays, feature walls, and architectural graphic elements fabricated using a range of materials including metal, acrylic, wood, and composite substrates.

Wayfinding & Navigation Systems

+

Comprehensive wayfinding solutions that guide movement through complex sites. Includes sign hierarchies, directories, maps, and ADA-compliant identification and navigation systems.

Exterior Identity & Entry Features

+

Exterior branding elements such as building markers, entry signage, façade graphics, and landmark features that establish identity and guide visitors.

Temporary & Event Graphics

+

Short-term graphics supporting promotions, events, campaigns, and transitions. Includes removable wall, window, and floor graphics designed for flexibility and fast deployment.

Renovations, Refreshes & Program Updates

+

Support for new construction, renovations, rebrands, and visual refreshes, including removal and replacement of existing signage and graphics as programs evolve.

Project Planning & Coordination

+

Site surveys, documentation, scheduling, fabrication coordination, logistics, and installation management. Our process is structured to support active environments with minimal disruption.
